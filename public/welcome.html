Axis

An independent editor for Fractal Audio devices — FM3, FM9, Axe-Fx III & AM4. Edit, back up, and organize your presets.

▶ Launch Axis in web Your device is plugged into this computer. No install — the browser talks to it directly over MIDI or USB. Best in Chrome or Edge on desktop. Firefox & Android work with MIDI devices (FM9, Axe-Fx III, AM4). ⇄ Launch Axis Remote Control the Axis desktop app running on another machine — from any browser, phone or tablet. Needs the Axis desktop app online with Remote enabled, and an Axis account.

Independent, third-party software. Not affiliated with Fractal Audio Systems. Use at your own risk — always back up your presets.
